    How to Use the JEE Main College Predictor

    1. Enter your JEE Main rank in the "Your Rank" field.
    2. Select your category from the "Your Category" dropdown.
    3. Select the type of college you are interested in from the "Type of College" dropdown.
    4. Choose your gender from the "Select Gender" options.
    5. Click on the "Predict My Colleges" button to see the eligible colleges.

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    What is the JEE Main College Predictor?

    The JEE Main College Predictor is a tool that helps you find the list of colleges you may be eligible for based on your JEE Main rank, category, and other preferences.

    At some ranks no college eligible is shown, Why?

    There could be several reasons why no colleges are shown as eligible for a particular rank:

    • High Rank: Your rank might be not in the range of previous year OR-CR range , so no college is filtered. you can approxiamte your rank by +/- 100 ranks to get better results.
    • Data Limitations: The predictions are based on the previous year's data (JoSAA 2023, 6th round). Admission trends and cutoffs can vary each year, and the current dataset might not cover all possible scenarios.
    • Filters: The filters you selected (category, gender, college type) might limit the number of eligible colleges. Try adjusting the filters to see if more options become available.

    If you believe there is an error or need more information, please feel free to contact us at support@collegenexus.

    How accurate is the prediction?

    The predictions are based on the data from JoSAA (2023) 6th round, and while they provide a good estimate, actual cutoffs may vary.

    Can I rely on this tool for my final college decision?

    This tool is intended to provide guidance. Always refer to the official sources and counseling sessions for final decisions.

    What if I encounter issues using the tool?

    If you encounter any issues or have any questions, please contact us at infincodes@gmail.com
